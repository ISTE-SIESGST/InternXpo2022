VirtuoSkill

Virtuoskill is a digital learning academy of Kalyanaraman Strategic Management Consultants (KSMC). KSMC is a learning and development partner for number of banks and Housing Finance Companies (HFCs) since its inception in Nov 2011. We are here with an avowed vision to skill human resources in mortgage finance sector in India to begin with. We work with a talented team of professionals with combined mortgage experience of more than 90 years in retail banking to build modules that learners can experience and practice in their professional work life.
