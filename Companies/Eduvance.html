Eduvance

Eduvance was founded by Ph.D. alumni from U.S. universities. They are dedicated towards providing participants with state of the art training in technology. They do this by introducing participants to projects and research topics that are at par with leading universities in the United States. This will make the participants technically sound and prepare them for future challenges. They assist participants write and present technical papers. They are advised by U.S. based researchers, faculty and industry professionals that have a proven track record in their field.

Available Roles:

  • Game Development
  • Internet of Things
  • Software Applications
